## üìñ About genogram-resources

Genograms are special family trees that show relationships and patterns across generations. They help therapists, social workers, and healthcare professionals understand family dynamics better.

The genogram-resources repository offers a set of useful tools, templates, and guides. This collection includes clear symbol guides, editable templates, and helpful tips for creating genograms. The resources target anyone involved in therapy, social work, counseling, or medical care who needs to visualize family histories.

This project saves you time and effort by gathering reliable genogram materials in one place. Whether you want to print templates, use digital tools, or study symbols, this resource has you covered.
